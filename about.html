About Us

Welcome to Opulent Bites We believe in serving the finest, freshest ingredients with a touch of passion. Our journey began with a love for flavors and a dedication to excellent hospitality. Enjoy a dining experience where taste and tradition meet!

Accomodation

The hotel offers 365 comfortable and well-equipped, air-conditioned guestrooms. Room facilities include CD player, climate control, clock radio, in-room safe, multi-line phone and voice mail. Iron/ironing boards are supplied in the rooms. Other room amenities include blackout drapes/curtains and minibar. Beds: Rooms at Opunent Bites Hotel boast premium bedding. Hypoallergenic bedding is also available. Hotel guests will be able to request a rollaway bed and a crib/cot (infant beds). Bathroom amenities include bathrobes, complimentary toiletries, hair dryer and makeup/shaving mirror. Entertainment: In-room entertainment options at Opunent Bites Hotel include flat-screen TV, pay movies, satellite television service and video-game console. Internet connection options: Business guests will appreciate dial-up Internet access, high-speed Internet access and wireless Internet access.Housekeeping services and complimentary newspapers are also available.

Facilities

Dining facilities at Opulent Bite Hotel include a restaurant. There is also room service available. The hotel boasts a 24-hour front desk service. A currency exchange facility is also available. Public areas at Opulent Bites Restaurant are comfortably air-conditioned. Hotel guests will appreciate the welcoming services of our multilingual staff and doorman. Other hotel amenities include gift shop/newsstand, hair salon and tennis court(s). Leisure amenities: There is an outdoor swimming pool at the hotel. Guests can also enjoy the following spa/wellness facilities: sauna, steam room, full-service health spa, spa tub. Other services: complimentary newspapers in lobby, computer rental, concierge desk, dry cleaning/laundry service, elevator/lift, express check-in/checkout, medical assistance available, porter/bellhop, safe-deposit box - front desk, supervised child care/activities, tour assistance and wedding services. Business/Internet: Opunent Bites Hotel features a well-equipped business center complete with audio-visual equipment and conference/meeting room(s). Event catering can also be arranged. The following Internet options are available at the hotel: wireless access; high-speed access in public areas; access in public areas is offered for a surcharge.
