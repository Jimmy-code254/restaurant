Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my reservation?

Yes, you can cancel up to 24 hours before check-in for a full refund.

Is breakfast included in all room bookings?

No, but you can add breakfast to your stay for an additional $10 per night.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer discounts for bookings of 5 or more rooms. Contact us for details.
